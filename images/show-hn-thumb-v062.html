prtr is not a prompt toy. It is the command layer between what you mean and what you should do next.

What it does

Build the first useful prompt, compare another app without rewriting context, and turn the answer into a patch, issue, plan, test, or summary prompt.

Why it exists

The annoying part of LLM work was never the first sentence. It was all the tab switching and repeat setup around it.
